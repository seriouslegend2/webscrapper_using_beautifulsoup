ACB will probe alienation of govt and endowments lands at Visakhapatnam involving YSRCP MP Vijayasai Reddy: AP endowments minister

TIRUPATI: Andhra Pradesh endowments minister Anam Ramanarayana Reddy said that the anti corruption bureau and an appropriate agency will soon launch a thorough probe into the alienation of govt and endowments lands worth several hundreds of crores of rupees at Visakhapatnam involving YSRCP Rajya Sabha MP V Vijayasai Reddy.
Speaking to reporters at Nellore on Thursday, Anam said that an internal enquiry by the endowments department has established the involvement of suspended assistant commissioner K Shanti and former govt advocate Subhash Reddy in numerous land scams at Visakhapatnam.

He elaborated that many government and endowment lands in Visakhapatnam were alienated at free-will with the help of Shanti and Subhash by Vijayasai Reddy in the last three years.
